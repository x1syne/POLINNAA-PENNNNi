# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Owners of dogs and cats in Pavlovsky Posad who want a calm grooming visit, clear contact options, real examples of work, and quick booking from mobile.

## Product Purpose

The site presents the GROOM salon, shows real grooming work, helps clients choose dog or cat care by breed, and moves them to Telegram, map, or booking without confusion.

## Brand Personality

Warm, careful, confident.

## Anti-references

No technical notes about exported chats, source files, implementation, placeholder pricing, or internal data structure. Avoid generic stock-only pages, heavy dark blocks, and oversized mobile text that overflows the viewport.

## Design Principles

- Real salon proof first: photos, videos, masters, address, and direct contact are more important than decorative copy.
- Mobile is primary: navigation, breed cards, media, and contact actions must fit comfortably on a phone.
- Rose and white carry the identity: pink is expressive, but text must remain readable.
- Keep choices clear: dog breeds, cat breeds, photos, videos, blog, and contacts should each have a simple place.
- Every media asset should be optimized for the web before shipping.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Use readable contrast, keyboard-friendly links and buttons, reduced-motion fallbacks, and layouts that do not require horizontal scrolling on mobile.
